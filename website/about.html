About Us

Founded by Arushi Dashore, MatchPoint.ai is a startup innovative web application, soon to extend to mobile platforms, designed for aspiring racket sport athletes in both recreational and competitive settings. Leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, it offers personalized coaching to its users. Our mission is to cultivate a passion for racket sports through accessible means, while also educating today’s youth about the transformative potential of machine learning.
